FULL PROJECT REPORT








Title of the Project:
Library Management System

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Introduction:

The Library Management System (LMS) is a browser‑based application designed to automate and simplify the management of books, members, borrowing, returning, due dates, and late fees. The system is implemented as a single HTML file using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. It does not require installation, a database, or a backend server. The LMS provides a clean, professional interface and includes demo data for immediate use.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Problem Statement:

Traditional library management relies on manual record‑keeping, which leads to:
Difficulty tracking borrowed and returned books
Errors in calculating due dates and late fees
Time‑consuming updates to records
Lack of centralized information
No automated history tracking

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Library Management System solves these issues by providing a digital, automated, and user‑friendly solution.

Objectives:

Maintain digital records of books and members
Automate borrowing and returning
Calculate due dates and late fees automatically
Provide editing and deletion of records
Maintain a complete history log
Offer a simple and professional interface
Reduce manual errors and improve efficiency.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Schools

Small libraries

Personal book collections

Features

-------------------------------

Book Management:

Add books
Edit books
Delete books
View all books
Track available and borrowed copies

------------------------------------------

Member Management:

Add members
Edit members
Delete members
View all members
Track borrowed books and late fees

----------------------------------------------

Borrowing:

Select member and book
Assign due date (7 days)
Reduce available copies
Add to history

---------------------------------------------

Returning:

Select member and book
Increase available copies
Calculate late fees
Add to history

---------------------------------------

Due Date and Late Fee Calculation:

Due date = 7 days from borrow date
Late fee = 1 currency unit per day

-------------------------------------------

History Log:

Shows borrow and return events
Includes date, time, and late fees

----------------------------------------------

Demo Data:
Includes Indian books and Indian member names.

--------------------------------------------------
